About Us

Northeast School is a highly rated, public school located in ITHACA, NY. It has 418 students in grades K-5 with a student-teacher ratio of 13 to 1. According to state test scores, 82% of students are at least proficient in math and 62% in reading. A critic says: "The best elementary school ever! I am so glad that I went here. The students are nice, the staff are professional, and the atmosphere is joyful." That is persuadable.

Achievements

Our legendary basketball game!

For 20 years we, the Northeast Eagles, were dominant. That was until the Northwest Legends appeared and were having a substantial winning streak, for 5 years straight. We lost against them a lot of times. We had to train really hard if we were to beat them. Then, it was the playoffs. We were going to lose because we were gonna play against those Legends. First, second and third quarters, they were leading, but as all those basketball fans say, it is neither the first nor second nor third quarter that matters, yet it was the last quarter, and that quote surely proved right! Every single one of the Eagles were shooting three pointers like crazy and we got 57-56. Northwest was leading! It was one more second into the game, and our lead Jackson gave us a lay-up, which gave us 2 points, and we won 1 ahead!!!

We won a blue ribbon!

Northeast Elementary School has very respectful teachers. They are skilled and make all the students feel like they matter. Our town mayor noticed our school and the teachers personality and found this remarkable. Therefore, he awarded us one blue ribbon! This was something we could never forget!!

We sucessfully won the state science fair!

Our school won the state's science fair! The child who won this was Arnold M who made some pretty neat robots out of legos and started talking about how much weight the LEGO Mindstorm EV3 large motors pull.
